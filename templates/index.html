What a Premium Domain Name Can
Do For Your Business
Instant Brand Recognition
A "premium" domain should be memorable and easy to spell, but such a domain is usually really expensive to get. Still, acquiring a premium domain is essential because it shapes the users' perception and tells them exactly what you're offering. You can imagine how this saves you a substantial amount of money and how it creates instant brand recognition. Just think about domains such as translators.com, cheapflights.com, etc. - they make the offer and value proposition very clear.
Gain Trust & Authority
Premium domains have another benefit - they give users a sense of trust and authority. If your domain is simple and memorable, your visitors will automatically have more trust in your business. The reason behind this is simple - online shoppers today understand how expensive it is to get such domains. That makes your brand much more reliable and trustworthy in their eyes and it leads to a higher conversion rate. Finally, with a good domain name at the base of your marketing strategy, this trust and authority will also increase the success of your other marketing channels.
SEO Benefits
A premium domain name can also improve your search engine rankings but not the way it was before. Google Exact Match Domain (EMD) algorithm doesn't rank exact match domains or keyword rich domains highly anymore. So, you can't just create a totally worthless website, get a great domain name and expect insanely good rankings. However, premium domain does have an SEO benefit and it's all about link acquisition. Trust and authority that come with a premium domain help your outreach and link placement which, in turn, improves your rankings.
Direct Traffic
Some premium domains drive huge amounts of direct type-in traffic. Those are the people who search for something and just type in the full domain name directly in the browser, skipping the search engine altogether. This usually leads to instant high-quality traffic and incredibly high conversion rates because these visitors already know what they want and they are ready to buy.
Physical Marketing Advantages
A premium domain name combines perfectly with all non-digital advertising strategies like TV, radio and print ads, billboards, etc. Physical advertising tactics combined with a short and memorable premium domain have amazing success rates. The users remember them much more easily and then visit the website when they're ready to buy.
